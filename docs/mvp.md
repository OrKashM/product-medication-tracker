## MVP Scope

The MVP focuses on providing a **simple, reliable backend system**
to track medication intake and manage stock automatically.

### MVP Features
- Medication stock management (quantity, form, unit)
- Treatment schedules (time and frequency)
- Intake confirmation (TAKEN / MISSED)
- Automatic stock decrement on confirmed intake
- Low-stock alerts
- Alerts for repeated missed intakes
- Role-based access (Patient / Caregiver)

### Out of Scope (Intentional)
- Medical advice or recommendations
- Automatic medication ordering
- Barcode or box scanning
- Advanced analytics or reporting
- Healthcare professional access

### Key Product Principles
- Backend as the single source of truth
- Business rules enforced server-side
- MVP prioritizes reliability over feature richness
